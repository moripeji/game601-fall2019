Rock-Paper-Scissors Modification: RPS Redeux
"RPS Redeux" is a new take on the classically-beloved game of Rock-Paper-Scissors, featuring modifications such as the implementation of playing cards, point systems, and an optional "Risk Round" in which players may place bets on their moves to increase their pool of points. Each modification in place in "RPS Redeux" specifically addresses the issues of fair play, lack of inclusivity, and lack of strategy found within the classic Rock-Paper-Scissors game. For more information, view the RPS Redeux rules and design document. "
