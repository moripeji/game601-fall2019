Rock-Paper-Scissors Modification: RPS Redeux
"RPS Redeux" is a new take on the classically-beloved game of Rock-Paper-Scissors, designed to specifically address issues of: 1) fair play, 2) lack of inclusivity, and 3) lack of strategy, all found within the classic game through the implementation of playing cards, a point system, and an optional "Risk Rounds" betting-based way of play. For more information, view the RPS Redeux rules and design document. "
